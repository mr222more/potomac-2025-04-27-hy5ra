About Potomac

Founded in 2010, Potomac emerged from a vision to revolutionize bathroom design through innovative shower solutions. Our journey began when our founders, experienced glass craftsmen, recognized the need for premium quality frameless shower doors that combined functionality with elegant design.

Our mission is to transform everyday bathrooms into luxurious retreats through exceptional craftsmanship and innovative design. Frequently Asked Questions

How long does installation take?

Professional installation typically takes 2-4 hours, depending on the complexity of your space. Our experienced team ensures minimal disruption to your home.

What warranty do you offer?

We provide a comprehensive lifetime warranty on all hardware and a 10-year warranty on glass and treatments, covering any manufacturing defects.

How do I clean and maintain the glass?

Our special coating makes cleaning simple - just use a squeegee after showering and occasional mild glass cleaner. No harsh chemicals required.

What types of glass options are available?

We offer clear, frosted, and various textured glass options in 3/8" and 1/2" thickness, all tempered for safety and durability.

Do you offer custom sizes?

Yes, all our shower doors are custom-made to your exact specifications, ensuring a perfect fit for any space.
